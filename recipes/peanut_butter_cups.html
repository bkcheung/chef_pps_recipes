Peanut Butter Cups

Super easy peanut butter cups!

5 easy ingredients combine to create this classic candy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dark chocolate
  • 1/4 cup + 1/2 tsp cocounut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • Muffin tin liner

Steps

  1. Line muffin tin with liners
  2. Add the dark chocolate and 1/4 cup coconut oil
  3. Stir in vanilla extract
  4. Microwave and stir until fully meled
  5. Pour half the mixture into the liners
  6. Freeze for 15 minutes
  7. In a measuring cup, add the peanut butter, honey, and 2 tsp coconut oil
  8. Microwave for 15s until melted
  9. Pour mixture into muffin tins
  10. Freeze for 5 minutes
  11. Pour remaining chocolate mixture on top
  12. Freeze for 1hr
  13. Enjoy!
